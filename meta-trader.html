Potential

A brokerage company — is an integral participant in international financial markets, acting as an intermediary between traders and stock exchanges. This rapidly growing type of business has changed significantly over the past 10 years. Trading has almost completely moved online, companies have become high-tech, and their service is more client-oriented. Another positive trend identified by marketing research is the ever-growing demand for services in the field of investment and online trading.
