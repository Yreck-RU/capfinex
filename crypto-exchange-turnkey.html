Потенциал

Биржа криптовалют – это онлайн-платформа для покупки, продажи, обмена и хранения криптовалют. Почти все биржи обладают широким функционалом и позволяют проводить большой спектр операций. Мы разработали несколько IT-решений для биржевых стартапов. Помимо собственной команды разработчиков мы сотрудничаем с компаниями из Европы, Азии и стран СНГ. Именно поэтому мы предлагаем не только SaaS, но и индивидуальную разработку проектов любой сложности.

Key products

Trading terminal

A large selection of terminals for brokers and cryptocurrency exchanges. Modern and intuitive design under your brand. Flexible functionality, hundreds of technical analysis objects, and compatibility with several APIs.

Administrative Portal

Back office and CRM for automation of management functions and interaction between company departments. Accounting and control of data. Sales level increase, marketing optimization and customer service improvement.

Investment platform

Modular platforms for trustful portfolio capital management. IT solutions for most types of investment companies. Adjust risk management conditions and distribute assets among managers.

Liquidity Aggregator

Single point of access to liquidity flows and smart order routing. Unlimited integrations with liquidity providers, prime brokers, and exchanges. Ensure minimum slippage and uninterrupted execution.

Customer portal

Multifunctional customer portal to register user accounts. Opening of trading accounts, identity verification and replenishment of balances. Monitoring and accounting of cash flow. High level of security and protection against hacking.

Market Data

Real-time financial data. A wide range of traded instruments, including currency pairs, cryptocurrencies, and securities. Access via the Unifeeder FIX or MT4/MT5 protocol.
